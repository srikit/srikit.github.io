At SriKIT we find solutions to complex problems in engineering and technology.

Everything we develop at SriKIT is open source, including all software, hardware, and mechanical designs.

SriKIT project is started in 2020 as an open-source hardware development group from its base in Sri Lanka.

At SriKIT, we design and develop open-source hardware kits and modules, provide engineering consultancy services and provide support to promote open-source technologies.

All our designs are produced on top of open source technologies and by using generally available components. It allows many people to access and test our designs with less expense and time.

Looks interested? Please join us and start to contribute to our projects.
